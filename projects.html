Mentality - Online Therapist Booking Website

Web Application

Feb 2020 - Apr 2020

Mentality is a booking enterprise solution where patients can book consultations with the therapists at our therapy booking web application. It is an online website that easily and conveniently connects people with our therapists based on their needs, with our doctors’ details such as specialisations and fees explicitly stated on the website. It consists of a landing page, patients' portal and doctors' portal.

This solution was built by a team of 5 for our Enterpirse Solution Development Module. The project allowed us to familiarise ourselves with the concepts taught in class such as interprocess communication (via messaging and REST APIs), front-end technologies (eg. Bootstrap, JQuery), as well as the use of containerisation in software development (via Docker). We challenged ourselves to go beyond what was taught in class by leveraging on external APIs (Stripe, Twillio). We also leveraged the cloud technology by deploying our docker containers on cloud and using Cloud RabbitMQ. 

Most importantly, beyond just applying different technologies, I was able to experience for the first time building an application from scratch, where we were able to set our own requirements and features for the application. It made me understood the importance of understanding the business requirements when building the application and making decisions in choosing the appropriate technologies to go with it.
